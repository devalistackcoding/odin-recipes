Lasagna

Description

One of the world's most beloved foods is finally here! This beautiful Italian lasagna has layers of slow-cooked ragù Bolognese and besciamella cheese sauce. Though it requires patience, it is quite straightforward to make, as you will see in the recipe video!

This reader favorite is included by popular demand in my debut cookbook, Dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 medium carrot
  • 1 celery stick
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1kg / 2lb beef mince

Steps

Ragu:

  1. Heat oil in a large heavy based pot over medium heat. Add garlic, onion, celery and carrots. Cook for 10 minutes until softened and sweet – they should not brown (if they do, turn heat down).
  2. Add beef, turn heat up and cook the beef, breaking it up as you go.
