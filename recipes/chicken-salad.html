Chicken Salad

Description

Chicken salads can be so dull… but not when it’s Vietnamese-style! This slaw-like Vietnamese Chicken Salad is everything you love about Vietnamese food: Fresh and bright, yet full of flavour. Tip: Don’t skimp on the herbs or peanuts. They really bring the dish to life with freshness and texture!

This is a reader-favourite recipe included by popular demand in my debut cookbook “Dinner”!

Ingredients

  • 350g/12oz cooked chicken
  • 6 heaped cups wombok cabbage
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1 red capsicum / bell pepper
  • 2 cucumbers
  • 1 large carrot
  • Continue reading...

Steps

  1. Dressing: Shake Dressing ingredients in a jar. Set aside 10 minutes to let flavours meld.
  2. Toss: Place all Salad ingredients in a very large bowl. Pour over half the Dressing and toss well. Set aside 5 minutes (veg will soften slightly, making it more "slaw-like").
  3. Toss again: Just before serving, toss again then add most of the remaining Dressing. Taste then add more Dressing if you want.
